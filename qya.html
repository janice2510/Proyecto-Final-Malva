What are the undies made from? By Kelly

The fabric body of the underwear is made from predominantly bamboo, with cotton and spandex for structure and stretch.The central leak proof gusset is a bamboo fabric that is polylaminated, which is then covered by a second layer of bamboo fabric. The absorbent styles have a central crotch section that is made from polyester (80%) and nylon (20%).The hipster waistband is made from nylon (52%), rubber latex (30%), and polyester (18%).
